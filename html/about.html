My Life Story PERSPECTIVE

My life is filled with Unique Perspective! When I was young, my Family and I Immigrated to the United States from Mexico City. As an immigrant, I have learned to face and surpass challenges such as Cultural and language Barriers. Consequently, I have developed unique abilities and Perspectives. Three valuable abilities I have developed as an immigrant include:

  • SYMPATHY towards my Audience and Peers
  • COMMUNICATION skills beyond Verbal
  • WORK ethic driven by a Vision/Dream


My Education BRIGHAM YOUNG UNIVERSITY
